Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Mileage Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

Mileage Tracker collects location data to enable automatic trip detection and mileage tracking. This includes:

  • GPS coordinates during trips
  • Starting and ending locations
  • Route information and distance traveled
  • Time and date of trips

Trip Information

We store information about your trips, including:

  • Trip purpose (business or personal)
  • Notes and descriptions you add
  • Odometer readings (if entered)
  • Work schedule settings

App Usage Data

We may collect basic usage information such as:

  • App settings and preferences
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports and diagnostics (anonymous)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide and improve the app's functionality:

  • Trip Tracking: To automatically detect, record, and categorize your trips
  • Report Generation: To create IRS-compliant mileage reports
  • Work Schedule: To automatically classify trips based on your work hours
  • Notifications: To alert you about detected trips and app activity
  • App Improvement: To identify bugs and improve app performance

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your location and trip data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your location data or trip information to any external servers. Your data stays completely private and under your control.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your trip data will be securely stored in your personal iCloud account and synced across your devices. This data is encrypted and protected by Apple's security measures. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored using iOS secure storage APIs
  • Location tracking uses standard iOS privacy protections
  • No third-party analytics or tracking services
  • No advertisements or data collection for marketing purposes

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information or location data to third parties. Your data remains private and is never transmitted outside of your device except as described in the iCloud Sync section above (which is optional and under your control).

Your Rights and Choices

Location Permissions

You can manage location permissions at any time through your iPhone's Settings app. Revoking location access will disable automatic trip detection but will not delete your existing trip data.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Individual Trips: Swipe left on any trip and tap Delete
  • All Data: Go to Settings > Delete All Data to completely remove all trips and reset the app
  • Uninstalling: Deleting the app from your device will remove all local data

Export Your Data

You can export all your trip data at any time in CSV, PDF, or JSON format from the Reports section.

Third-Party Services

Mileage Tracker does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely on your device.

Apple Services

The app uses Apple's built-in services:

  • Core Location: For GPS tracking (subject to Apple's privacy policies)
  • iCloud: For optional data sync (if enabled by you)
  • StoreKit: For in-app purchases (subject to Apple's privacy policies)

Children's Privacy

Mileage Tracker is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not sell your personal information and all data is stored locally on your device, most CCPA provisions do not apply. However, you always have the right to request deletion of your data (which you can do directly in the app).
