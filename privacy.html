Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Essential Dev Team ("we," "our," or "us") operates the MileTracker mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: MileTracker collects location data to track your driving trips and calculate mileage. This includes:

  • GPS coordinates during trips
  • Start and end locations of trips
  • Route information

Motion Data: We collect motion and fitness data to detect when you start and stop driving, enabling automatic trip detection.

Trip Information: Information you enter about your trips, including:

  • Trip categories (Business, Medical, Charity, Personal)
  • Notes and tags
  • Vehicle information
  • Odometer readings

Account Information: If you subscribe to premium features, Apple processes your payment. We receive a subscription status but not payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the MileTracker service:

  • Track and record your driving trips
  • Calculate mileage and tax deductions
  • Generate mileage reports
  • Sync data across your devices (if enabled)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All trip data is stored locally on your device by default. We do not have access to your local data.

iCloud Sync: Premium subscribers who enable iCloud sync will have their data stored in their personal iCloud account. This data is encrypted by Apple and accessible only to you.

We Do Not:

  • Store your data on our servers
  • Have access to your trip history
  • Sell or share your location data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your trip data remains on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or property
  • With your explicit consent

5. Your Rights and Choices

Location Permissions: You can control location access through your device settings. Disabling location access will prevent automatic trip tracking.

Data Deletion: You can delete all your trip data at any time through the app's Settings. Deleting the app will also remove all local data.

iCloud Data: If you use iCloud sync, you can manage or delete this data through your iCloud settings.

Export: Premium subscribers can export their data in standard formats (CSV, PDF) at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

MileTracker is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

MileTracker uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple MapKit: For displaying maps and routes
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing subscriptions
  • Apple CloudKit: For optional iCloud sync

These services are governed by Apple's privacy policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@essentialdevteam.com
  • Website: essentialdevteam.com

10. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to delete your personal information. Since we store data locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times.
