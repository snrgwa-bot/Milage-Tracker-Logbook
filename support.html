Support & FAQ

Find answers to common questions and get help with Mileage Tracker.

Getting Started

How do I enable automatic trip detection?
To enable automatic trip detection, go to Settings and toggle on "Auto Detection". Make sure to grant location permissions when prompted. The app needs "Always" location access to detect trips in the background.
Why does the app need location permissions?
Mileage Tracker needs location access to automatically detect when you're driving and record your trips. Your location data stays on your device and is never shared with third parties. We require "Always" permission to detect trips even when the app is in the background.
How do I set up my work schedule?
Go to Settings and tap on "Work Schedule". Select your work days and set your start and end times. The app will use this information to automatically classify trips as business or personal based on when they occur.

Tracking Trips

How accurate is the automatic trip detection?
The automatic trip detection uses GPS and motion sensors to accurately identify when you're driving. It filters out non-driving activities like walking or biking. Trips are typically detected within seconds of starting to drive.
Can I manually add or edit trips?
Yes! You can manually add trips by tapping the "+" button on the Trips screen. You can also edit any trip by tapping on it and selecting "Edit". This lets you adjust the purpose, distance, notes, and other details.
What should I do if a trip isn't detected?
If a trip isn't automatically detected, you can manually add it. Make sure auto-detection is enabled and that you've granted "Always" location permissions. Very short trips (under 0.1 miles) may not be detected to avoid false positives.
Can I continue a previous trip?
Yes! If you stop briefly during a trip, you'll receive a notification asking if you want to continue the previous trip or start a new one. You can choose to continue the same trip or start fresh.

Reports & Export

How do I generate an IRS-compliant report?
Go to the Reports tab and select the date range you want to report. The app will generate a detailed report including:
  • Trip dates and times
  • Starting and ending locations
  • Distance traveled
  • Business purpose
  • Total business vs. personal miles
You can export this report as a PDF or CSV file.
What export formats are available?
Mileage Tracker supports multiple export formats including CSV (for spreadsheets), PDF (for printing and archiving), and JSON (for data backup). Choose the format that works best for your needs.
Can I export data for tax purposes?
Absolutely! The app generates IRS-compliant reports that include all necessary information for claiming mileage deductions. Export the report as a PDF and keep it with your tax records.

Battery & Performance

Will this app drain my battery?
Mileage Tracker is optimized to minimize battery impact. It uses efficient background monitoring that only activates when you're actually driving. Most users report minimal battery impact with typical daily use.
How much storage does the app use?
The app itself is small, and trip data is stored efficiently. Even with years of trip history, the app typically uses less than 50MB of storage. You can always export and delete old trips to free up space.

Privacy & Security

Is my location data private?
Yes! All your location and trip data is stored locally on your device. We don't upload your location data to any servers or share it with third parties. Your privacy is our top priority.
Can I delete my data?
Yes, you can delete individual trips or all your data at any time. Go to Settings and use the "Delete All Data" option to completely remove all trips and settings from your device.
